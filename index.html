José Gutiérrez is a UX Designer, he designs solutions together with clients and users. With his studies in Communication, José is involved in the digital strategy of organizations, creating solutions that permeate not only the interface, but the organization and how it communicates with every interaction with its users.

Furthermore José has been a great promoter of the Web community in general of Costa Rica by creating and nurturing communities like (Web Costa Rica, Drupal Costa Rica, UX Costa Rica) as well as helping several global communities, such as: IAI (Information Architecture Institute) in its participation as Social Media director WIAD 2016 (World Information Architecture Day) and volunteer UXPA2016 (User Experience Professional Association Conference 2016). José also has helped organise events such as WIAD Costa Rica and Drupal Camp Costa Rica.
